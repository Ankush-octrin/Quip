About Me

I’m a developer and creator who believes that everyone has experiences worth sharing. I created Quip as a place where people can share their funny, memorable, creepy, unexpected, or everyday experiences in their own words.

Quip is for everyone, including those who may sometimes feel alone or simply want someone to listen. Because every experience deserves to be heard, and sometimes, sharing a moment with a stranger can make the world feel a little less lonely.
